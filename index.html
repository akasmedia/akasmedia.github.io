The Beginning

Posted on July 5th, 2019

AkasMedia started out as a facebook advertising agency back in early 2019.
Now the company has scaled up to doing advertising on many more platforms such as Instagram, Snapchat, Whatsapp and Linkedin.
Business has been great ever since but websites was where the business really took off.
Every company doesn't need social media marketing to stay alive (yet) but, they do need some sort of online presence so a standard business website is a must .

The Visionaries

In the beginning, there were two brilliant men. They both were looking for something more to do than the regular 9-5 hustle. They wanted more and so they researched day in and out. One day, the youngest of the two mentioned social media marketing just as he had heard it from his virtual mentor. Then a bulb sparked in the mind of the eldest of the two. He then mentioned to the younger one, why not use social media marketing for our business I'm sure you can learn that. Indeed he did learn it and then formed the company AkasMedia from it!

Progression was always Key

In the world of money what we were making wasn't much money. The good thing is that we saw this business' potential. In essence, feeding our business was what made much of a difference. We didnt't buy fancy clothing or bragged to others how nice our life was. We just kept it simple. Once the business grew more annually then we were satisfied because, now our end goal seemed closer than it was last year.

The End Game

In the end, what we really wanted to do was be with our real family. Setting up the infrastructure of where we belong. Helping our home become the magnificent place it should be.
